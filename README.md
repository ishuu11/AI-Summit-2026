# AI Summit – Educational Trip Media Archive 🎓🤖

This repository contains **photos and videos captured during the AI Summit Educational Trip**, documenting student participation, technology showcases, learning sessions, and memorable moments.

This educational trip was organized for **2nd Year students of Vaish College of Engineering**, aiming to provide early exposure to real-world applications of modern and emerging technologies.

---

## 📌 About the Educational Trip

The **AI Summit Educational Trip** was conducted with the objective of:
- Introducing **2nd year students** to industry-level technologies
- Bridging the gap between **theoretical knowledge and practical exposure**
- Encouraging innovation, curiosity, and professional growth among students

The trip played a significant role in helping students understand how technologies like **Artificial Intelligence** are applied in real-world scenarios.
